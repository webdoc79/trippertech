About Us

Tripper Technologies Pvt Ltd (TTPL) is in the business of providing GLOBAL CONNECTIVITY via its flagship product TripperWiFi, a Smart and Sim-Free, IoT (Internet of Things) MiFi Device.

TripperWiFi’s unique Virtual Sim Technology connects you to a LOCAL NETWORK of the country/ies being visited. This reduces costs of International Roaming, thus allowing the device to provide Unlimited Data Access in more than 130 countries at the most Affordable rates. All this comes with a host of other features too.

TTPL aims to be a pioneer in offering tailor-made solutions to the world of Internet of Things (IoT) from concepts to completion.

Besides TripperWiFi, TTPL has many other upcoming products in the pipeline.

TTPL was started with an objective of assisting the Travel Industry to enhance their earnings and the Corporates to save big on their international roaming bills. This way the travel agents and corporates can aid their clients and employees with a cost effective and futuristic method of Staying Connected, Globally 24/7.

THE INITIATORS

Manan Parikh and Manish Bagadia initiated the inception of Tripper Technologies Private Limited. Sharing their interests in travel, IoT, technology, telecommunications and SaaS - related products led them to explore opportunities in these fields.

Riding the entrepreneurial journey together since awhile now, the duo forayed into an unexplored territory in India with no roadmaps or guidelines to follow. Just a clear vision! Having a thorough understanding of what it takes to be successful, the two sides of the same coin – Manan and Manish, leveraged their individual strengths and worked on various aspects that needed development. All this and more was worked on with an intent to provide optimum results that add value to the travel and tourism industry as well as help corporates upgrade and ease the lives of their clientele and employees.

Our Portfolio

TRIPPERWIFI


It’s now time to "Say Goodbye to SIM cards, Bill Shocks, Limited Countries, Limited Data Plans and High International Roaming Costs."

TripperWiFi a Smart IoT and Sim-Free Pocket-Sized MiFi device powered by Virtual Sim Technology that automatically connects to a LOCAL NETWORK in more than 130 countries, providing unlimited and uninterrupted data access. Each TripperWiFi Device can simultaneously connect up to 10 users/ devices. TripperWiFi is an apt solution for personal use or group travel.

Travel Agents Digital & Marketing Shop


The importance of taking a business digital has increased manifold over the years. The Digital Shop is an initiation by TTPL to take travel agents digital by creating a one-stop shop platform.

With the help of this product, travel agents will be able to reach a larger number of B2B and B2C segments with extreme ease and uniqueness.

TRIPPER Travellers Connect App


Tripper Travellers Connect is an interactive white-labelled app for Travel Agents and their clients. This app will make travelling a smoother and novel experience with all the information the clients may require for the entire duration of the travel simply on the click of a button.

TRIPPER TALK


Tripper Talk is a communication-centric mobile application with a VoIP service that facilitates international calling and messaging on other mobile and landline numbers across the globe, using your existing mobile number or by purchasing a different country’s virtual number at very low costs and date usage.
